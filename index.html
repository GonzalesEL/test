Asuka Kazama


Asuka Kazama is a fighter from Tekken. Introduced in Tekken 5 from 2005, she is a brash high-school student and a practitioner of Kazama Style Traditional Martial Arts. She is known across Osaka for breaking up fights in the neighborhood by fighting them hand-to-hand.

Lightning Mcqueen


Lightning McQueen is a former racer previously sponsored by Rust-eze (now by Dinoco) in the Cars franchise. He used to be very obnoxious towards others and only has his eyes on the Piston Cup. After getting into Radiator Springs however, McQueen became more humble and helped others rather than solely seeking the Piston Cup.

Ryuhei Sawamura


Ryuhei Sawamura, a.k.a The Owari Dragon, is a featherweight and junior lightweight boxer from Hajime No Ippo. He is an extremely cruel counter-puncher that toys with his opponents rather than going for a knock-out and occasionally fouls, as shown in his fight against featherweight JBC champion Makunouchi Ippo.

Rei Ayanami


Rei Ayanami, a.k.a The First Child, is a character from the infamous Neon Genesis Evangelion. She has a stoic personality, not showing emotion for her peers Shinji Ikari and Asuka Langley Soryu (which is why the latter hates her). Rei pilots Evangelion Unit-00 to fight against Angels with her peers.

Humpty Alexander Dumpty


Humpty Dumpty was the main antagonist of the first Puss in Boots film. He was very close friends with Puss and would talk about the Golden goose. This friendship would fall apart when Humpty lies to Puss so that he could help rob the San Ricardo bank, which fails and gets Humpty to jail.
